About me

I am a research scientist at Deepmind. I completed my D.Phil studies at University of Oxford supvervised by Nando de Freitas. Before that I obtained my masters degree at University of British Columbia and my undergraduate degree from University of Waterloo in computer science.

My main research area is machine learning. Currently, I am focusing my research on Deep (Reinforcement) Learning. In my previous life, I mostly worked on improving Bayesian optimization.

Interests

  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning
  • Reinforcement Learning
